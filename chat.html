Why Microelectronics?

Chips power everything from smartphones to spacecraft. At NAU, we are training the next generation of engineers to solve critical shortages in the semiconductor supply chain. Our facility offers industry-grade tools for lithography, deposition, and metrology.

Hands-on Fabrication

Don't just simulate circuits, build them. Our curriculum emphasizes physical processing. You will learn to gown up, handle wafers, spin coat photoresist, and etch patterns using industry-standard equipment.
