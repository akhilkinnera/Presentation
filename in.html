MPCT at a glance

3 Pillars

Processing, characterization, and testing built into a single pathway.

Hands-on

Lab-first training with safety, documentation, and measurement discipline.

Industry-ready

Workflows aligned to manufacturing, metrology, and reliability needs.

Explore MPCT pillars

Processing

Cleanroom fundamentals, lithography concepts, materials, and process flow documentation.

Characterization

Optical and surface metrology, microscopy, dimensional analysis, and structured reporting.

Testing

Electrical verification, reliability principles, and repeatable measurement procedures.

Workforce Pathways

Internships, capstones, lab practicums, and portfolio artifacts aligned to hiring signals.

Why MPCT matters

Microelectronics needs technicians and engineers who can follow process discipline, produce defensible measurements, and communicate results. MPCT is designed to train that exact profile.
